Hotkeys

MediaKeyPlayPause native
Toggles between play and pause
MediaKeyPreviousTrack native
Switches to previous track
MediaKeyNextTrack native
Switches to next track
MediaKeyLaunch native
Activates Google Music tab (despite settings).
